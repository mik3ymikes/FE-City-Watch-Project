Statistics

According to FBI 2022 Released Data

There were 380.7 violent crimes per 100,000 people reported to the FBI from law enforcement agencies in 2022.

The property crime rate in 2022 was estimated at 1,954.4 incidents per 100,000 people.

Robbery showed an estimated increase of 1.3% nationally.

FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center received a total of 800,944 reported complaints, with losses exceeding $10.3 billion.

Safety Tips and Reccomendations

  • Install security systems
  • Exercise caution when out of town
  • Lock doors and close windows/blinds during night hours
  • Establish a neighborhood watch program
  • Ensure there is adequate lighting
  • Create an emergency preparedness plan
  • Don't hesitate to report unusual activity
